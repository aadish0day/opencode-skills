# Natural Transitions

Phrases that move readers smoothly between sections without feeling mechanical. Use sparingly — one per section break is enough.

## Problem → solution
- "So how do you fix it?"
- "Here's what changes that."
- "The good news: it doesn't have to be this way."

## Idea → proof
- "Don't take our word for it."
- "Here's what that looks like in practice."
- "Teams like yours are already seeing it."

## Section → next section
- "Now, the part that surprises most people…"
- "That's the 'what.' Here's the 'how.'"
- "Which raises the obvious question:"

## Objection → reassurance
- "Worried about the switch? You're not alone."
- "And if it's not a fit, that's fine too."

## Closing → CTA
- "Ready to see it for yourself?"
- "The fastest way to find out is to try it."
